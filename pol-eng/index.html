Political Engineering

This site is a work-in-progress. It is intended to be a reference to on-line resources related to the topic of political engineering, which is the still rather immature discipline of designing political institutions deliberately to embody certain values and achieve certain desired kinds of systemic behavior while minimizing the risk of subversion or other undesirable behaviors. I will be filling it out over time; please feel free to send links and suggestions to me at "baford" (at) "mit" (dot) "edu".
