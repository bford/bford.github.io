Electoral Systems

Proposed/Experimental Systems

  • Weighted Delegate Councils
  • Local Balance Representation: A scheme to encourages parties to form across ethnic boundaries and hence help to avoid civil wars in highly volatile regions.
  • Interactive Representation: A variation of Single Transferrable Vote where excess votes of seat winners are not transferred but instead affect the winner's voting power on the council.
  • Direct Representation: A more ambitious proposed variation of STV, further along the lines of interactive representation, geared toward giving voters exceptionally broad choice of candidates in electing large weighted councils.
